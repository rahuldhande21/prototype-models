Advance Booking Scheme

Alias: A Token Booking · Pre-season customer advance

Scheme Details
Configuration & integration info
In Use
Name of SchemeAdvance Booking Scheme
AliasA Token Booking
SystemSAP
Integration withGlass Run & other systems
CompaniesMahyco, Leadbeter
PurposeBooking product in advance to get discount during delivery
How it works
Customers book product quantity in form of SKU by giving a token amount per packet/unit/etc. They receive a rebate as a credit note when the product is delivered during the season.
Process flow
1 · Customer pays token amount (per SKU)
↓
2 · Product booked in advance via SAP/Glass Run
↓
3 · Product delivered during season
↓
4 · Rebate issued as Credit Note to customer
Example Calculation
📋 Sample — Arjun Agro · Cotton ABC · Jan 2026

Rebate (credit note)₹50
Balance due at delivery₹650
Formula: 900 − 200 (token) − 50 (rebate) = ₹650

Quantity Discount

Alias: After Season Scheme · Volume-based rebate for customers

Scheme Details
In Use
NameQuantity Discount
AliasAfter Season Scheme
SystemSAP
IntegrationMahyco Scheme Application
CompaniesMahyco, Leadbeter
PurposeIncrease product sales; discount % per quantity sold (state/region-wise)
How it works
A different scheme type defined to increase product sales. Discount percentages are defined according to quantity sold by the customer. It includes state-wise and region-wise definitions.
Process flow
1 · Qty slab discount % defined (state/region-wise)
↓
2 · Discounts calculated in Mahyco Scheme Application
↓
3 · Credit Note posted after calculation
↓
4 · Customer-wise posting in SAP via standard TCode
ℹ️ Discount slabs vary by state and region. Final calculation is performed in the external Mahyco Scheme Application and then posted back into SAP. Monitor integration reliability regularly.
